Down the Hatch

You are an adventurer: a rugged, tough warrior who explores the dangers of a mysterious world to sate your curiousity and goldlust. One day, while exploring some ancient ruins, the ground gives way beneath you and you fall! You lose consciousness when you hit the ground- but you're not dead yet. Eventually you wake up...

Press Enter to begin!

Help

Type what you want to do into the text box and press enter to play.

Commands:
North, East, South, or West: Move in the indicated direction if there's an exit that way.
Get or Take (Item): Pick up an item in the same room. For example: get sword.
Drop (Item): Drop an item in the same room. For example: drop sword.
Use (Item): Use an item to do something.
Inventory: List the items in your inventory.
